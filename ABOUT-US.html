THE CATAMAYA

beautiful 65 foot custom built day charter vessel

The largest and better equipped Catamaran in the Riviera Maya

we believe that life is in the detail

With vast amounts of shaded seating areas with cushions, sun seated areas as well as huge nets to lay on and watch the Caribbean Sea lapping beneath you.  Lets not forget the hard sundeck roof area too! 

We have 3 bathrooms all accessible by stairs not ladders (Great for all ages!) We also have 2 full size double grills aboard for a tasty meal to be prepared freshly on board, not to forget the built in water slide, the only one of this kind in the Caribbean!

Starting the tour at the catamaran sails for about an hour to the INAH reef. While the client enjoys the scenery, music and fishing. The crew will provide all passengers with safety instructions and snorkeling equipment will be provided. The snorkeling activity lasts around 45 minutes and depends on weather conditions. While the group is snorkeling, the cook will prepare the previously selected lunch accompanied by garnishes and drinks from the national open bar. Finishing lunch, the catamaran will sail back to Puerto Aventuras, the group can enjoy entertainment with the crew.

COVID-19

Management of COVID 19

In order to protect the health and safety of our customers, we are pleased to share with you our standards and protocols that CATAMAYA applies to comply with the health standards requested by the government, according to the current traffic light.
 
Catamaya staff has a certificate to ensure knowledge of the operating protocols for COVID19, as well as the necessary hygiene measures such as hand washing, use of face masks, healthy distance, use of sanitizing gel among other important measures to preserve health.

Our facilities have sanitization certification for all areas, paying more attention to common areas with high human traffic in order to effectively combat the virus. We have several areas of sanitizing gel placed in strategic areas for the spindle of our staff and clients.
 
In the same way, the transportation has a cleaning and sanitization kit for the use of the clients, the passengers are seated in an interspersed way, in such a way that they can respect the healthy distance. (In private trips this measure does not apply), for our drivers The use of a mask is mandatory throughout the journey as well as the disinfection of all areas of the transport.
